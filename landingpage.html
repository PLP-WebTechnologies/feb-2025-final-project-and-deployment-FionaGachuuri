About Eco-Connect

Eco-Connect is a community-focused platform designed to connect you with your neighbours in the pursuit of a clean and sustainable environment. Together, we can identify opportunities for improvement and recognize the positive environmental practices already in place. Eco-Connect fosters a sense of shared responsibility, ensuring a healthy and thriving environment for everyone.

Eco-Connect Features

Login, Users enter their credentials to be able to report issues




View issues, Users can be able to view reported issues




Report an issue, Users can report environmental issues in their community



Report an Issue

Report any environmental issues in your community and help us keep our environment clean and safe.

What inspired Eco-Connect

Eco-Connect was born from the need to address escalating environmental pollution within communities. It provides a solution by offering a central platform where individuals can easily report local environmental problems,gain knowledge, and collaborate with neighbors to foster collective action and awareness.
